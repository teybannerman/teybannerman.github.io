A growing catalogue of AI products, practical research, and initiatives that are bringing meaningful positive change to the world.

---

The loudest AI stories are about productivity, efficiency, and automation. The most important ones are about purpose.

The Purposeful AI Project is my attempt to catalogue and celebrate the AI work that matters most — systems that expand access to healthcare, tools that protect vulnerable communities, research that makes AI more equitable, products that solve problems people actually have.

This isn't a ranking or an award. It's a growing, curated collection of evidence that AI can be built with intention — and that the people doing this work deserve more attention than they're getting.
